About Me

I took on new job responsibilities related to basic JavaScript and DOM manipulation for site merchandising, marketing, UX enhancements and site optimization. This sparked my curiousity so I began taking a formal JavaScript course to better understand the fundementals of the language. Hello, world! Career Path

A quick summary of my career path so far: I went to a top ranked public business school where my focus was on marketing, sales, and digital & social analytics. After graduation, I moved to NYC to work as a site merchandiser and category specialist for macys.com. A few years later I helped them patch gaps as they moved tech operations from San Fransico to NY. In this role I managed our countdown timer execution, site testing, Q4 execution, competitive analysis and other responsibilities. In the most recent role, I have been managing an A/B testing workflow for all areas of the site funnel with an Adobe Experience Cloud tool - Adobe Target. Within this role I work with developers on converting business requirements into testable features. To supplement this work, I began working through the Udemy course. I plan to keep building my programming skills by adding projects to this portfolio, leveraging more frameworks, integrating APIs, and applying clean coding principles.
