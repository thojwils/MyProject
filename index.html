Career Path

I went to a Indiana University - Kelley School of Business for my bachelor's degree. Here my focus was on marketing, sales, and digital & social media analytics. After graduation, I began my time at Macy's Inc. as a site merchandiser and category specialist for macys.com Big Ticket upholstery department. After an organizational transformation, I took on a new role and responsibilities related to broader macys.com digital strategy. In this role I managed our countdown timer execution, event driven marketing, website quality assurance & testing, Q4 execution, key competition digital analysis and other responsibilities. In my current most role, I manage an A/B testing workflow that impacts all components of the site funnel via the Adobe Experience Cloud tool - Adobe Target. The tool allows for JavaScript to be served to selected audience segments via several activity types. Within this role I work with developers on converting business requirements into testable features. The current pipeline of experimentation and optimization calls for interesting scripts, strong virtual collaboration, establishing intuitive processes and a genuine curiousity for learning new skills and understanding the competitive technical landscape that drives modern global applications. I plan to keep building my programming skills by adding projects to this portfolio, refactoring when necessary, integrating APIs, and applying clean coding principles. Always striving for incrementality and finding ways to keep things simple, functional, and fun!
